I'm Stephanie, the owner and photographer of Magnolia Coasts
Photography. I'm the woman behind the camera making magic happen by
creating custom sets, wrapping and hushing babies to sleep and making
children laugh.
Hello! I’m Stephanie.

As a photographer, I’ve been in the business 11 years and have 14+ 
years working with newborns. I’ve photographed thousands of families, 
couples, and babies across the GTA, many of them several times over, 
including families with 7+ children!

This business has never just been about photos. It’s about 
relationships. It’s about watching your family grow, being invited 
back into your lives, and capturing the in-between moments that 
make your story.

A little about me…

I married my university sweetheart, have been together 23 years 
together (which still feels a little surreal to say). We have two 
teenagers, 12 and 14, who keep life busy, active and running the 
roads, and our big 5-year-old white fluffy dog who is always along 
for the ride.

We’ve travelled and lived all over Canada, from the east coast to the 
west coast, and I think that’s shaped how I see people and connection. 
I’m always drawn to beauty in the everyday, especially flowers. 
Magnolias in particular have followed me through different seasons 
of my life, and they’ll always hold a special meaning for me.

I also love to travel, and I bring a piece of that inspiration back 
with me every time, whether it’s in the way I use light, notice 
details, or tell a story through images.

If you’ve worked with me before, you know how much I value what 
we build together. And if you’re new here, just know, you’re not just
booking a session. You’re starting a relationship.

And that means everything to me
